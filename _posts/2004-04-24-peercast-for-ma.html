PeerCast What is PeerCast? PeerCast is a new, free way to listen to radio and watch video on the Internet. It uses Peer-to-Peer technology to let anyone become a broadcaster without the costs of traditional streaming. This means you get to hear and watch stations not normally found on commercially funded sites.
