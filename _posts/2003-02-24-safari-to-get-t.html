Think Secret - Safari to get tabbed browsing (with images) February 24, 2003 - Apple's Safari web browser will soon come equipped with a tabbed browsing feature, Think Secret has confirmed. New pre-release betas that are being tested include this capability, one of the most anticipated additions to the popular upstart browser.
